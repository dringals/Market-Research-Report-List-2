<p><p>Blood group typing is a crucial aspect of blood transfusion and organ transplant procedures. In the market, there are four main types of techniques used for blood group typing. PCR-Based and Microarray Techniques utilize polymerase chain reactions and chromosomal microarrays to determine blood types accurately. Massively Parallel Sequencing Techniques employ high-throughput sequencing to quickly identify multiple blood groups. Assay-Based Techniques use specific antibodies or antigens to detect blood types. Lastly, Other techniques include molecular assays, serological methods, and advanced technologies that contribute to comprehensive and precise blood group typing.</p></p>
