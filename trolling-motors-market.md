<p><ul><li>Bow Mount</li><li>Transom Mount</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Trolling motors are classified into two main market types: bow mount and transom mount. Bow mount trolling motors are designed to be mounted at the front, or bow, of a boat. This positioning allows for more accurate steering and control, especially in windy conditions. On the other hand, transom mount trolling motors are affixed at the back, or transom, of a boat. They provide simplicity and convenience, making them easier to install and maneuver. Both types offer different advantages and suit different boating needs and preferences.</p></p>
