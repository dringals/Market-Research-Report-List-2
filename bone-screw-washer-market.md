<p><p>A bone screw washer is a medical device used in orthopedic surgeries to ensure the proper fixation and stability of bone screws. It is often used in conjunction with bone screws in procedures such as fracture fixation, spinal fusion, and joint reconstruction. The washer is placed between the bone and the head of the screw to distribute the pressure evenly, prevent loosening, and minimize the risk of bone damage.</p><p>The bone screw washer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of bone-related injuries and disorders, along with the rising elderly population, is driving market growth.
