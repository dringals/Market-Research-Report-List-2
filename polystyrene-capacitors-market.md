<p><p>Polystyrene capacitors are electronic components widely used for their stability and low loss in high-frequency applications. There are two main types: axial polystyrene capacitors and radial polystyrene capacitors, which differ in their physical construction. Axial polystyrene capacitors have two leads extending from each end and are usually used in applications where they need to be mounted vertically, while radial polystyrene capacitors have leads on the same side and are typically used in horizontal mounting. Both types offer excellent electrical characteristics, making them suitable for various electronic devices and circuits.</p></p>
