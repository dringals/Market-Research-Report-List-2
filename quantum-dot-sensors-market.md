<p><strong>The Quantum Dot Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistance Strain Quantum Dot Sensors</li><li>Piezoresistive Quantum Dot Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quantum dot sensors are categorized into two main types: resistance strain quantum dot sensors and piezoresistive quantum dot sensors. Resistance strain quantum dot sensors utilize the change in electrical resistance of a quantum dot material when subjected to strain or pressure, allowing for precise measurement of mechanical forces. On the other hand, piezoresistive quantum dot sensors employ the piezoresistive effect, where the electrical resistance of the quantum dot material changes in response to applied mechanical stress. Both types offer accurate and efficient measurement of strain or pressure in various applications.</p></p>
