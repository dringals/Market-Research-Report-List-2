<p><p>The microphone power supply market can be broadly categorized into three types: 12V, 48V, and Others. The 48V power supply, on the other hand, offers a higher voltage for microphones that require phantom power, necessary for condenser microphones to function optimally. The "Others" category covers any other power supply voltages that are not 12V or 48V, catering to specific microphone models with unique power requirements.</p></p>
