<p><p>Smart container tracking systems are used to monitor the movement and location of containers during transportation. The market types include GPS, cellular, LoRa WAN, BLE, and others. GPS utilizes satellites to provide accurate positioning information, while cellular systems use mobile network technology. LoRa WAN uses low-power wide area networks for long-range communication. BLE, or Bluetooth Low Energy, enables short-range wireless communication. Other market types may include technologies such as RFID or NFC. These systems enhance container security, optimize logistics, and improve supply chain management.</p></p>
