<p><p>The cotton spinning market encompasses various types of spinning techniques. Ring spun refers to a traditional method that produces fine and strong yarn. OE/Rotor spun utilizes an open-end spinning system suitable for producing bulkier yarns. Core spun involves wrapping cotton fibers around a core of synthetic or natural fibers for enhanced strength and durability. Air jet spun involves high-speed jets of air to twist the fibers together, while frictional spun utilizes friction to create yarn. Additionally, there are other spinning techniques available in the market, offering different characteristics and applications.</p></p>
