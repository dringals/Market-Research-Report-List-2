<p><p>The Aircraft Cargo System market consists of two main types: Cargo Loading Systems and Interior Cargo Fittings. Cargo Loading Systems refer to the equipment used to load and unload cargo onto an aircraft. This includes loaders, conveyors, and various handling devices necessary for efficient cargo operations. On the other hand, Interior Cargo Fittings refer to the components inside the aircraft's cargo area that ensure the secure and organized storage of cargo during flight. These may include pallets, nets, and dividers to prevent shifting and damage to the cargo.</p></p>
