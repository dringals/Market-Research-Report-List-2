<p><p>Emergency stop buttons are critical safety devices used to quickly halt machinery or equipment in emergency situations. They are available in various market types, including Null Line and Live Line Type and Single Live Line Type.</p><p>In Null Line and Live Line Type, the button is connected to both null line and live line wires, ensuring complete disconnection of the power supply when pressed. This double disconnection provides enhanced safety.</p><p>On the other hand, Single Live Line Type emergency stop buttons are connected only to the live line wire. When pressed, they disconnect the power supply, halting the machinery. While not as safe as Null Line Type, they are still effective in many applications.</p></p>
