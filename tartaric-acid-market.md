<p><p>Tartaric acid market consists of three types: L-Tartaric Acid, D-Tartaric Acid, and DL-Tartaric Acid. L-Tartaric Acid is the purest form, derived from natural sources, and used in various industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. D-Tartaric Acid is the mirror image of L-Tartaric Acid and also finds applications in similar industries. DL-Tartaric Acid is a racemic mixture of L and D forms, offering a cost-effective alternative with lesser purity. Different market players cater to these types, offering versatile solutions to meet specific industry requirements.</p></p>
