<p><strong>The IR Windows Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crystal Material</li><li>Polymer Material</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>IR windows market types can be divided into crystal material and polymer material markets. Crystal material IR windows are made from materials like zinc selenide or germanium, which offer high infrared transmittance and durability. These windows are suitable for industrial applications with harsh conditions. On the other hand, polymer material IR windows are made from materials like polyethylene or polyvinyl chloride, offering a cost-effective solution for general applications. These windows provide good infrared transmittance but may have lower durability in comparison to crystal material windows.</p></p>
