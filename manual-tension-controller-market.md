<p><p>Manual tension controllers are used in various industries to maintain consistent tension levels in materials such as paper, foil, and fabric. Open loop control is a type of manual tension control where the operator adjusts the tension setting based on experience and visual feedback. Close loop control, on the other hand, utilizes sensors to measure the actual tension and automatically adjusts the setting to maintain a desired tension level, providing more accurate and precise control. Both types of market cater to different user requirements, with open loop control being more cost-effective and close loop control offering higher accuracy.</p></p>
