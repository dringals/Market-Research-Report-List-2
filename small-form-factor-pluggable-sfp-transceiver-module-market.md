<p><p>The Small Form-factor Pluggable (SFP) Transceiver Module market includes two main types: Singlemode Fiber and Multimode Fiber. Singlemode Fiber offers higher data transmission over longer distances, making it suitable for long-haul communication. It uses a thinner core that allows only one mode of light to travel through. On the other hand, Multimode Fiber is designed for shorter distances and uses a larger core that allows multiple modes of light to propagate. These different market types cater to specific needs and requirements of diverse communication networks.</p></p>
