<p><p>In the UHP Gas Cabinets market, there are three main types: Manual, Semi-automatic, and Fully Automatic. </p><p>- Manual gas cabinets require human intervention for all operations, including opening and closing valves and monitoring gas flow. </p><p>- Semi-automatic gas cabinets automate certain functions, such as controlling valves or monitoring gas levels, but still require human intervention for others.</p><p>- Fully automatic gas cabinets operate independently and can perform all necessary functions without human intervention. They utilize advanced technology to control valves, regulate gas flow, monitor levels, and ensure safety measures are in place.</p></p>
