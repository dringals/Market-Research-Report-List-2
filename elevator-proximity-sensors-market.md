<p><p>Elevator proximity sensors play a crucial role in ensuring safety and efficiency in elevators. The market for these sensors includes multi-beam door sensors and ultrasonic door sensors. Multi-beam door sensors use multiple beams of light to detect obstructions and ensure doors do not close on passengers. Ultrasonic door sensors detect the presence of objects or individuals using sound waves, preventing accidents and ensuring smooth operation of the elevator. Both types of sensors contribute to the overall safety and performance of elevators.</p></p>
