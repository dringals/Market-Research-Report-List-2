Diodes allow current to flow in only one direction, while IGBTs provide high voltage and current capabilities. MOSFETs are used for switching and amplifying electronic signals. Bipolar transistors control electrical current by amplifying or switching it. Thyristors are semiconductor devices that allow current flow after their gate is triggered. These devices together serve different functions in power electronics applications.</p></p>
