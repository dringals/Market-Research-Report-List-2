<p><p>Diamond band saw blades are classified into two main types: toothless type and tooth type. Toothless type blades have a continuous rim without any teeth, which allows for smoother cutting of brittle and delicate materials like glass and ceramics. These blades are suitable for precision cutting applications. On the other hand, tooth type blades have diamond-coated teeth spaced along the rim. These blades provide more aggressive cutting action and are commonly used for cutting hard materials like stone and concrete. The choice of blade type depends on the specific cutting requirements and the material being cut.</p></p>
