<p><ul><li>One Piece Bag</li><li>Two Piece Bag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ileostomy products market is divided into two main types, namely one-piece bag and two-piece bag systems. The one-piece bag consists of a flange and a pouch that are permanently attached, offering a simplified and compact design. On the other hand, the two-piece bag consists of a separate flange and pouch, allowing for easier pouch changes without removing the flange. Both options provide a secure barrier to collect ileostomy output, but the two-piece bag offers more flexibility and convenience for individuals requiring frequent pouch changes.</p></p>
