<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal infrared detector market consists of three main types of detectors: microbolometer IR detectors, thermopile IR detectors, and pyroelectric IR detectors. Microbolometer IR detectors rely on a temperature-sensitive resistor to detect infrared radiation. Thermopile IR detectors use multiple thermocouples to measure temperature differences caused by infrared radiation. Pyroelectric IR detectors detect changes in the electrical polarization of pyroelectric materials due to infrared radiation. These detectors are widely used in various applications such as surveillance, automotive safety, thermography, and industrial inspection.</p></p>
