<p><p>The lyophilized antivenins market includes different types of antivenins, such as neurotoxin antivenins, blood toxin antivenins, and mixed toxin antivenins. Neurotoxin antivenins are designed to neutralize the venom produced by neurotoxic organisms, such as snakes and spiders, that can cause paralysis or nervous system damage. Blood toxin antivenins are developed to counteract the toxins found in the venom of hemotoxic organisms, like certain snakes, scorpions, or spiders, which can cause damage to blood cells and tissues. Mixed toxin antivenins are formulated to treat venoms containing both neurotoxic and hemotoxic components.</p></p>
