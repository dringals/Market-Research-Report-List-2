<p><p>Subsurface Safety Valves (SSVs) are crucial components used in oil and gas wells to prevent uncontrolled flow of hydrocarbons. The market for SSVs can be divided into two types: Ground Control Safety Valves and Subsurface Control Safety Valves. Ground Control Safety Valves are located above ground and are used to shut down a well in case of emergencies. On the other hand, Subsurface Control Safety Valves are installed below ground and are designed to control flow from a well to the surface, ensuring safety and preventing blowouts.</p></p>
