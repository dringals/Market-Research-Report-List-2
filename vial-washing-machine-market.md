<p><p>The Vial Washing Machine market consists of two main types: Linear Tunnel Type and Rotary Type. Linear Tunnel Type machines are designed as a continuous system where vials move linearly through a tunnel, ensuring a high-speed and efficient washing process. On the other hand, Rotary Type machines have a rotating mechanism where vials are placed on a rotary table and washed through a series of rotating nozzles. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different production capacities and requirements in the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries.</p></p>
