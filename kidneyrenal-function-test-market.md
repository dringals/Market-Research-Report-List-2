<p><p>The Kidney/Renal Function Test Market comprises two main types: Urine Test and Blood Test. Urine tests examine the urine to measure various elements like protein, glucose, and red/white blood cells. This helps assess kidney health and detect any abnormalities. Blood tests, on the other hand, evaluate the blood for creatinine, urea, and other markers of kidney function. Both tests assist in diagnosing kidney diseases, monitoring ongoing conditions, and determining suitable treatment plans. These tests play a crucial role in assessing overall kidney health, identifying potential issues, and managing patients' care effectively.</p></p>
