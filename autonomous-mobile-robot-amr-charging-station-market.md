<p><p>In the Autonomous Mobile Robot (AMR) Charging Station market, there are two main types: wireless AMR charging stations and physical contact-based charging stations. Wireless AMR charging stations utilize wireless technologies to transfer power from the station to the AMR without the need for direct physical contact. On the other hand, physical contact-based charging stations require the AMR to be physically connected to the charging station through cables or other physical means to transfer power. These different types cater to different needs and preferences within the AMR market.</p></p>
