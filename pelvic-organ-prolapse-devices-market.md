Vaginal Mesh, on the other hand, is a surgical implant used to support the pelvic organs. It is made of synthetic material and is permanently implanted through a surgical procedure. Both devices aim to alleviate symptoms and provide support to the pelvic organs for women with pelvic organ prolapse.</p></p>
