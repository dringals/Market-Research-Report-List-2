<p><strong>The Power Quality Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Phase</li><li>Three Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power quality meters are devices used to measure and monitor the quality of electrical power. There are two main types of power quality meters: single-phase and three-phase meters. </p><p>Single-phase meters are designed to measure the power quality parameters in single-phase electrical systems, which typically provide power to residential and small commercial buildings. These meters track various parameters such as voltage, current, power factor, harmonics, and voltage sags or swells to ensure the quality and efficiency of the power supply.</p><p>Three-phase meters, on the other hand, are used in larger commercial and industrial settings where three-phase electrical systems are present. They can monitor and analyze multiple phases simultaneously, providing a comprehensive understanding of power quality. These meters also measure parameters such as current imbalance and voltage dips, helping to identify and address issues that may impact the smooth functioning of electrical equipment.</p></p>
