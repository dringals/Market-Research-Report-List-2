<p><p>The combustion control equipment market is segmented into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic control equipment. Manual control equipment requires human intervention to monitor and adjust the combustion process. Semi-automatic control equipment combines manual control with some level of automation, such as automatically adjusting certain parameters. Fully automatic control equipment involves complete automation, where the system continuously monitors and adjusts all aspects of combustion without human involvement. These different types cater to varying levels of control and monitoring requirements in different industrial processes.</p></p>
