<p><p>Refrigerant valves serve an important role in controlling the flow of refrigerants within cooling systems. There are several types of refrigerant valves available in the market. Electric valves are operated through an electric actuator, providing precise control over refrigerant flow. Electromagnetic valves use an electromagnetic field to regulate refrigerant flow. Manual valves require manual adjustment by turning a handle or lever. Pneumatic valves are controlled by compressed air or gas. Each type of valve offers different benefits and can be selected based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
