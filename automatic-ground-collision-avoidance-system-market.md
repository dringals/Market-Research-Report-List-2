Two types of Auto GCAS are ACAS and ASAS. ACAS stands for Airborne Collision Avoidance System, which primarily uses sensors on the aircraft to detect and avoid potential ground collisions autonomously. ASAS stands for Airborne Surveillance and Advisory System, which provides advisory information to the pilot regarding possible ground collisions, allowing them to take appropriate preventive actions. These systems enhance flight safety and reduce the risk of accidents.</p></p>
