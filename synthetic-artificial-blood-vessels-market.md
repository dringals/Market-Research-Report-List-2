They are used in various surgical procedures, including bypass surgeries, dialysis access, and coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG). </p><p>The global synthetic artificial blood vessels market has been experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases and related risk factors such as hypertension and obesity. These advancements have resulted in improved patient outcomes and reduced post-operative complications. Moreover, the increasing preference for minimally invasive procedures is driving the demand for synthetic artificial blood vessels, as they offer less invasive techniques compared to traditional open surgeries.</p><p>The market is witnessing various trends, including a shift towards bioresorbable vascular grafts that can gradually be replaced by natural tissue, reducing the risk of complications associated with permanent synthetic implants.
