<p><strong>The Hall-Effect Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threshold Type</li><li>Linear Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hall-Effect Sensors market can be categorized into two main types: Threshold Type and Linear Type. Threshold Type sensors are designed to detect when a magnetic field crosses a predetermined threshold, triggering a response. Linear Type sensors, on the other hand, provide a linear output that corresponds directly to the strength of the magnetic field being detected. Each type has its own unique applications and advantages, making them versatile tools for a wide range of industries such as automotive, electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
