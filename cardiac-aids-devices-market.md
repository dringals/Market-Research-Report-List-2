<p><p>The Cardiac Aids Devices market consists of various types of devices used to assist and support the functioning of the heart. Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) are mechanical pumps that assist the weakened or failing heart in pumping blood. Full Artificial Hearts are complete replacement devices that mimic the natural heart's functions. Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs) are used to increase blood flow to the heart by inflating and deflating a balloon placed inside the aorta. Other devices include implantable cardioverter-defibrillators, pacemakers, and external counterpulsation systems. These devices aim to improve heart function and save lives.</p></p>
