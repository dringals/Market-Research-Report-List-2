<p><p>Cholesterol testing kits can be classified into two main types: analyser kits and test strips kits. Analyser kits are equipped with a specialized device that measures the cholesterol levels in the blood sample. These kits can provide precise and quantitative results. On the other hand, test strips kits involve using a test strip that is dipped into a blood sample and then inserted into a reading device. These kits provide a visual indication of cholesterol levels based on color changes on the test strip.</p></p>
