<p><p>Additive Manufacturing Equipment with Metal Powders refers to the technological process of creating three-dimensional objects by adding successive layers of metal powders. The market types for this equipment include Selective Laser Melting (SLM), Electronic Beam Melting (EBM), and Other market. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) utilizes a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powders, while Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) uses an electron beam to achieve the same purpose. The "Other" market category includes different additive manufacturing techniques that employ metal powders, such as Binder Jetting and Material Extrusion. These processes have revolutionized the manufacturing industry by offering increased design flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
