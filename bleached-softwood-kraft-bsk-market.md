<p><p>Bleached Softwood Kraft (BSK) refers to a type of pulp made from softwood trees that has been bleached. There are two main market types for BSK: Southern Bleached Softwood Kraft (SBSK) and Northern Bleached Softwood Kraft (NBSK). The Southern variant is known for its strength and brightness, making it ideal for high-quality printing and writing papers. On the other hand, the Northern variant is recognized for its purity and high tear strength, making it suitable for packaging materials and tissues.</p></p>
