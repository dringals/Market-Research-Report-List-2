<p><strong>The Encoders for Industrial Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Relative Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Encoders for industrial robots are devices that convert mechanical motion into electrical signals to provide accurate position feedback. Absolute encoders provide a unique digital code for each position, allowing precise control and avoiding position loss during power interruptions. On the other hand, relative encoders measure position changes relative to a reference point, making them more cost-effective and suitable for applications that don't require absolute positioning. Both types play crucial roles in the industrial robot market, offering different advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
