<p><p>Thermopile IR sensors are used for detecting and measuring infrared radiation. There are two main market types for Thermopile IR sensors: Through Hole and SMD (Surface Mount Device). Through Hole sensors require pins for soldering and are commonly used when strong connections are necessary. SMD sensors, on the other hand, are smaller in size and are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them suitable for compact designs. These market types cater to different preferences and requirements based on factors such as size, ease of installation, and connection strength.</p></p>
