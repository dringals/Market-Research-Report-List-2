<p><p>In the enterprise market, various types of augmented reality (AR) technologies are gaining traction. Head-mounted displays (HMDs) are worn like goggles and provide immersive AR experiences, allowing users to interact with virtual objects in their field of view. Smart glasses are lightweight eyewear that overlay digital information onto the wearer's real-world view, enabling hands-free information access and communication. Head-up displays (HUDs) project relevant data onto a transparent display, allowing users to see the information without averting their gaze from the environment. These AR solutions hold immense potential for enhancing productivity, training, and collaboration in the business sector.</p></p>
