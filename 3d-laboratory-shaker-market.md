<p><ul><li>Tilting</li><li>Rocking</li><li>Orbital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D laboratory shaker market offers three main types of shakers: tilting, rocking, and orbital. Tilting shakers provide a rotating motion on a single axis to mix samples in containers. Rocking shakers move in a back-and-forth motion, allowing thorough mixing without splashing. Orbital shakers rotate horizontally in a circular motion, enabling efficient mixing in various container sizes and volumes. These different types cater to specific laboratory requirements and applications, providing researchers with a range of options to achieve optimal sample mixing and agitation.</p></p>
