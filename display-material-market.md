<p><p>The display material market encompasses various types, including polarizers, substrates, color filter layers, liquid crystals, backlighting units (BLU), and others. Polarizers enhance display visibility by reducing glare and improving contrast. Substrates provide structural support and electrical connectivity. Color filter layers allow the display to produce vibrant colors. Liquid crystals control the passage of light to create images. Backlighting units illuminate the display from behind. Other materials include adhesives, resins, and films that aid in the production and functionality of LCD displays. Overall, these materials play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and quality of electronic displays.</p></p>
