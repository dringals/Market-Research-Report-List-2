<p><p>Ceramic inserts refer to cutting tools used in machining processes. They are available in two types: single-crystal ceramics and polycrystalline ceramics. Single-crystal ceramics are made from a single crystal structure, allowing for exceptional hardness and wear resistance. On the other hand, polycrystalline ceramics are made from multiple crystal structures, providing good toughness and shock resistance. Both types of ceramic inserts are widely used in industries such as automotive and aerospace for their high-performance characteristics in cutting and drilling applications.</p></p>
