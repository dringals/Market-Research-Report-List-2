<p><strong>The Trailed Mixers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Trailed Mixers</li><li>Horizontal Trailed Mixers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trailed mixers are agricultural equipment used for mixing and feeding livestock feed or forage. There are two main types: vertical trailed mixers and horizontal trailed mixers. Vertical trailed mixers have a vertical auger that mixes the feed vertically and discharges it from the bottom. They are ideal for processing large quantities of feed and are commonly used in dairy farms. On the other hand, horizontal trailed mixers have a horizontal auger that mixes the feed horizontally and discharges it from the side. They are suitable for smaller operations and are commonly used in beef or sheep farms.</p></p>
