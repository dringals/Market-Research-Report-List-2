<p><p>The high-end beef market is primarily categorized into two types: grainfed beef and grassfed beef. Grainfed beef refers to cattle that are raised on a diet mainly consisting of grains like corn, soy, and barley. This type of beef typically has a higher marbling and tenderness due to the increased fat content. On the other hand, grassfed beef refers to cattle that are exclusively fed on grass and forage throughout their lives. This type of beef generally has a leaner texture and a distinct grassy flavor.</p></p>
