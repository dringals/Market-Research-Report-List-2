<p><p>Utility drones can be categorized into two main types: multi-rotor and fixed-wing drones. Multi-rotor drones are equipped with multiple rotors that enable them to hover in the air and maneuver in any direction, making them ideal for tasks that require precise control and hovering capabilities, such as aerial photography or inspections. On the other hand, fixed-wing drones resemble mini airplanes, with wings that provide lift and allow them to cover large distances quickly and efficiently. These drones are commonly used for tasks that require long-range surveillance, mapping, or cargo delivery.</p></p>
