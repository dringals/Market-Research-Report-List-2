<p><p>The vision care products market comprises two main types: spectacles and contact lenses. Spectacles, commonly known as eyeglasses, are frames fitted with lenses that correct vision problems such as nearsightedness or farsightedness. They come in various styles, shapes, and materials to cater to individual preferences. Contact lenses are thin discs placed directly on the eye's surface to correct vision. They offer greater convenience for active individuals, provide a wider field of view, and are available as daily disposables, monthly or extended wear lenses. Both spectacles and contact lenses aim to improve visual acuity and enhance overall eye health.</p></p>
