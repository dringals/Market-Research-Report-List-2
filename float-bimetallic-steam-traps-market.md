Thread type steam traps are designed to be screwed onto the piping system, allowing for easy installation and removal. On the other hand, flange type steam traps are bolted onto the piping system using flanges, providing a more secure and airtight connection. Both types offer efficient condensate drainage and are commonly used in industrial processes that require the removal of condensate from steam systems.</p></p>
