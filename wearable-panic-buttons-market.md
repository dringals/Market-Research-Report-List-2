These devices are becoming increasingly popular among the elderly, individuals with medical conditions, and those who work in high-risk environments.</p><p>The wearable panic buttons market is expected to exhibit steady growth in the coming years. Factors driving this growth include increasing awareness about personal safety and security, rising elderly population, and the need for prompt emergency response systems.
