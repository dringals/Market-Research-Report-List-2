<p><strong>The Distributed Denial-of-Service(DDoS) Protection Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UDP Flood</li><li>ICMP Flood</li><li>SYN Flood</li><li>HTTP Flood</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) Protection Services market includes various types of protection mechanisms against different types of DDoS attacks. UDP Flood protection safeguards against User Datagram Protocol (UDP) flood attacks where the target's system is overwhelmed with a large number of UDP packets. ICMP Flood protection defends against Internet Control Message Protocol (ICMP) flood attacks, which flood the target's system with ICMP packets. SYN Flood protection guards against SYN flood attacks by preventing the exhaustion of system resources. HTTP Flood protection shields against HTTP flood attacks that exploit vulnerabilities in web servers. "Others" refer to additional types of DDoS attacks that may require specialized protection measures.</p></p>
