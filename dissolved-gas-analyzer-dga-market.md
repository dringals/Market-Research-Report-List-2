<p><strong>The Dissolved Gas Analyzer (DGA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Extraction or Rack Method</li><li>Head Space Extraction Methed</li><li>Stripper Column Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolved Gas Analyzer (DGA) market offers various types of analysis methods including Vacuum Extraction or Rack Method, Head Space Extraction Method, and Stripper Column Method. Vacuum Extraction or Rack Method involves extracting gases from transformer oil using vacuum pumps. Head Space Extraction Method analyzes gases that have passed from the oil to the headspace above the oil. Stripper Column Method involves stripping gases from transformer oil using a column packed with a suitable adsorbent material. These methods are widely used in the power industry for monitoring and diagnosing transformer health.</p></p>
