<p><p>Twisted cable, also known as twisted pair cable, is a type of cable that consists of two separately insulated conductors that are twisted together in a helical form. The market is witnessing innovations like the introduction of shielded twisted pair (STP) cables that provide enhanced protection against electromagnetic interference.</p><p>The market also experiences certain trends that are shaping its growth.
