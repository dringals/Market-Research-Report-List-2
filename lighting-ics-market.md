<p><strong>The Lighting ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC-DC LED Driver IC</li><li>DC-DC LED Driver IC</li><li>LED Dimming Driver IC</li><li>Linear LED Driver IC</li><li>Ballast IC</li><li>NFC IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lighting ICs are electronic components used in lighting systems to control and regulate various functionalities. AC-DC LED Driver ICs convert alternating current to direct current for driving LED lights, while DC-DC LED Driver ICs regulate the voltage supplied to LEDs. LED Dimming Driver ICs enable adjustable brightness levels, Linear LED Driver ICs ensure constant current output, and Ballast ICs control the power supply to fluorescent or HID lamps. NFC ICs facilitate wireless communication and control between lighting systems and mobile devices. These market segments cater to different lighting requirements and offer a wide range of solutions.</p></p>
