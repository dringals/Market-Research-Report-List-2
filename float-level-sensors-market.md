The magnetic float level sensors work through a principle of magnetism, where the position of a magnetic float changes based on the liquid level, and this change is detected by a sensor. Mechanical float level sensors, on the other hand, use a mechanical mechanism to detect the liquid level based on the movement of a float. Other types of float level sensors may use different technologies such as ultrasonic or capacitive to determine the liquid level.</p></p>
