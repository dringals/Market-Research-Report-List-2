<p><p>The stone machinery market consists of several types of machines used in the stone industry. Bridge Saw Machines are designed for cutting slabs of stones and making straight or curved cuts. Multi Cutters are versatile machines used for cutting, shaping, and polishing stones. Crushers are used for crushing large stones into smaller pieces. Lastly, there are other miscellaneous machines used in the stone industry for various purposes.</p></p>
