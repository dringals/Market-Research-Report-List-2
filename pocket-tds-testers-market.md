<p><p>Pocket TDS (total dissolved solids) testers are used to measure the concentration of dissolved ions in water. These testers are available in both high range and low range market types. High range TDS testers are designed to measure the concentration of dissolved ions in water with a higher concentration, typically above 1000 parts per million (ppm).
