<p><p>Digital onboarding software refers to tools that streamline the customer onboarding process. Cloud-based solutions operate on remote servers, enabling users to access and manage the software via the internet. This type offers easy scalability and flexibility, as well as automatic updates and backups. Web-based onboarding software, on the other hand, is installed on local servers and accessed through a web browser. This option provides more control and data security, but may require additional IT infrastructure and maintenance. Both markets cater to businesses looking for efficient onboarding solutions, with cloud-based being more convenient and web-based offering more control.</p></p>
