<p><p>A wireless microphone is a device that is used to amplify sound without the need for a physical connection between the microphone and the sound system. These advancements are attracting consumers, propelling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the market is experiencing a shift towards the adoption of wireless microphone systems with multiple channels.
