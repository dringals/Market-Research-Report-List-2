These switches are commonly used in mainlines, yards, and sidings in the railroad network.</p><p>The steel track switch market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. <p><strong>The Steel Track Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Turnout</li><li>Equilateral Turnout</li><li>Three-Way Turnout</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel track switch market consists of various types, including single turnout, equilateral turnout, three-way turnout, and others. A single turnout is a basic type with one divergent route, while an equilateral turnout has two divergent routes with equal angles. A three-way turnout has three divergent routes, allowing trains to change direction or continue on different tracks. The "others" category refers to less common or specialized switch types, such as diamond crossings or double slips. These different types cater to the diverse needs and configurations of railway systems.</p></p>
