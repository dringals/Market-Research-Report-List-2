<p><strong>The Portable Power Sprayers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Coupling Type</li><li>Belt Driven Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Power Sprayers market consists of two main types: Direct Coupling Type and Belt Driven Type. </p><p>Direct Coupling Type power sprayers operate by directly coupling the engine to the pump, which allows for efficient power transmission and minimal energy loss. This type is preferred for its compact design, lightweight, and ease of portability.</p><p>On the other hand, Belt Driven Type power sprayers use a belt system to transfer power from the engine to the pump. This type offers increased flexibility in pump speed adjustment and is suitable for applications requiring varied pressure and flow rates. It is typically used in more heavy-duty or commercial applications.</p></p>
