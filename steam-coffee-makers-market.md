<p><p>Steam coffee makers can be divided into two main market types: full-automatic and semi-automatic. Full-automatic steam coffee makers are designed to automate the brewing process entirely. They typically have programmable settings for different coffee varieties and can grind the beans, heat the water, and extract the coffee with minimal user intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic steam coffee makers require more manual control and intervention from the user. They usually have manual controls for grinding, tamping, and extracting the coffee, allowing the user to customize the brewing process according to their preferences.</p></p>
