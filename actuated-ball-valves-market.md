<p><ul><li>Electrically Actuated Ball Valves</li><li>Pneumatically Actuated Ball Valves</li><li>Air Actuated Ball Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The actuated ball valves market comprises three main types: electrically actuated ball valves, pneumatically actuated ball valves, and air actuated ball valves. Electrically actuated ball valves use an electric motor to control the valve's opening and closing actions, providing precise automation. Pneumatically actuated ball valves employ compressed air to actuate the valve, offering fast and reliable operation. Air actuated ball valves use air pressure to actuate the valve, suitable for applications where electricity is not available. These types of valves find wide usage in various industries for fluid control and automation purposes.</p></p>
