<p><p>The Atomic Absorption Spectrometer market is categorized into three types. Flame Atomic Absorption Spectrometer involves atomizing the sample using a flame, while Graphite Furnace Atomic Absorption Spectrometer uses a graphite tube to atomize the sample at a higher temperature. Integrated Atomic Absorption Spectrometer combines both flame and graphite furnace techniques for improved analysis. These instruments are utilized in various industries for elemental analysis, determining trace metal concentrations, and quality control purposes. The market for these spectrometers is driven by their accurate and sensitive analysis capabilities, aiding in scientific research and industrial processes.</p></p>
