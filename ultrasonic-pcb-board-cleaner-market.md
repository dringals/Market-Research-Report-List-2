<p><p>The Ultrasonic PCB Board Cleaner market can be classified into two types: Portable and Benchtop. Portable cleaners are compact and lightweight, making them easy to transport and suitable for small-scale or on-the-go cleaning applications. On the other hand, Benchtop cleaners are larger and stationary, designed for heavier cleaning tasks and high-volume production environments. Both types utilize ultrasonic technology to generate high-frequency sound waves that create microscopic bubbles in a cleaning solution, effectively removing dirt, grease, and contaminants from PCB boards.</p></p>
