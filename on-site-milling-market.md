<p><p>On-Site Milling market encompasses various types of milling processes used in different industries. Linear milling involves the use of a machine that moves along a linear axis to mill surfaces and edges. Gantry milling utilizes a larger machine that moves along multiple axes, enabling milling on larger workpieces. On the other hand, orbital milling involves the use of a specialized machine that rotates around a fixed axis to mill circular or curved surfaces. These different market types offer versatile solutions for industries requiring on-site milling services.</p></p>
