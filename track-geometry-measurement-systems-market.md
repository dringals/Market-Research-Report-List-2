<p><strong>The Track Geometry Measurement Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Track Geometry Trolley</li><li>Track Geometry Inspection Vehicle (TGIV)</li><li>Autonomous Track Geometry Measurement System (ATGMS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Track Geometry Measurement Systems are used in the railway industry to assess the condition of tracks. The market includes three types: Track Geometry Trolley, which is a manual system pushed along the tracks by workers; Track Geometry Inspection Vehicle (TGIV), a motorized system that can cover larger distances and collect data more efficiently; and Autonomous Track Geometry Measurement System (ATGMS), a fully automated system that uses sensors and algorithms to collect data without any human intervention. Each system varies in terms of mobility, data collection capabilities, and level of automation.</p></p>
