<p><p>The semiconductor spin coater equipment market is divided into two main types - fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic spin coater equipment requires minimal human intervention and performs all the processes automatically, from dispensing the coating material to spinning and drying the substrate. On the other hand, semi-automatic spin coater equipment requires some manual operations, such as loading and unloading the substrate, but still automates the spinning and drying processes. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of semiconductor manufacturers for their coating operations.</p></p>
