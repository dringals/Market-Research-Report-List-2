<p><p>The Hybrid Fiber Coaxial (HFC) market consists of various types of equipment and components. Transceivers enable data transmission in both directions over the HFC network. Amplifiers boost the signal strength to prevent degradation over long distances. Encoders convert digital signals for transmission over the HFC network. Modulators convert electrical signals into optical signals for transmission over the fiber optic cables. RF combiners combine multiple RF signals into a single output. Splitters divide the signal into multiple outputs. Optical nodes convert optical signals back into electrical signals. Fiber optic cables transmit data using light pulses, while coaxial/copper cables transmit data using electrical signals.</p></p>
