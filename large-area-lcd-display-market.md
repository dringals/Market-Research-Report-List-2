<p><p>The Large Area LCD Display market can be divided into three types: Static, Simple Matrix, and Active Matrix. </p><p>Static displays are mainly used for information display purposes, such as in digital signage. </p><p>Simple Matrix displays use a simple row and column configuration and are commonly seen in calculators or digital watches. </p><p>Active Matrix displays are sophisticated and use thin-film transistors to control each pixel individually, offering high resolution and color quality. These displays are widely used in smartphones, tablets, and televisions.</p></p>
