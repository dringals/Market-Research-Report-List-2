<p><p>Automated Parcel Delivery Terminals (APDTs) are self-service machines that provide a convenient alternative for parcel delivery and pickup. These terminals are strategically located in various locations such as shopping malls, office complexes, public places, and residential areas. APDTs are equipped with lockers of different sizes, barcode scanners, and touch screens for users to interact with. Customers can select a locker, place the parcel inside, and receive a unique code or barcode to access the locker when picking up the parcel.</p><p>The market for APDTs has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. Increasing e-commerce activities and the growing demand for convenient parcel delivery options have been driving the market growth. Additionally, the rise in urbanization, busy lifestyles, and the need for secure and safe delivery has further catalyzed the adoption of APDTs.</p><p>The market is also witnessing various trends. One of the major trends is the integration of APDTs with smart technology and IoT capabilities. APDTs are also being equipped with advanced technologies such as facial recognition, biometrics, and artificial intelligence to improve the overall user experience and security.</p><p>Another trend is the expansion of APDT networks in emerging economies.
