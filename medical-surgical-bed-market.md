<p><p>The medical surgical bed market can be categorized into two types: motorized and non-motorized beds. Motorized beds are equipped with electric motors that allow height adjustment, backrest inclination, and leg elevation with the push of a button, enhancing patient comfort and facilitating caregiver tasks. Non-motorized beds, on the other hand, are manually operated and require physical effort to adjust their height or position. Both types serve the same purpose of providing a safe and comfortable environment for patients during medical procedures or post-surgery recovery.</p></p>
