<p><p>Electric roll-up doors are widely used in various industries, including commercial, industrial, and residential sectors. There are two types of electric roll-up doors available in the market - wall-mount rolling doors and floor-mount rolling doors. Wall-mount rolling doors are installed directly onto the building's wall and are suitable for areas with limited floor space. Conversely, floor-mount rolling doors are mounted on the floor and are an ideal choice when there is no available wall to accommodate the door. Both types of doors provide convenient and secure access control while maximizing space utilization.</p></p>
