<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Developer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Developer</li><li>Negative Photoresist Developer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photoresist developer market can be categorized into two types: positive photoresist developer and negative photoresist developer. Positive photoresist developers are chemicals used to remove the exposed regions of a positive photoresist material, leaving behind the desired pattern. In contrast, negative photoresist developers are used to dissolve the unexposed regions of a negative photoresist material, resulting in the desired pattern. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry for creating high-resolution patterns during the fabrication process.</p></p>
