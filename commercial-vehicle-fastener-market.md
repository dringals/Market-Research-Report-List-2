<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Fastener Market is classified into two types: Threaded Fasteners and Non-threaded Fasteners. Non-threaded Fasteners, on the other hand, include rivets, clips, pins, and washers that do not have threads for attachment. These fasteners play a crucial role in commercial vehicles by ensuring the structural integrity and reliability of various components, thereby enhancing vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
