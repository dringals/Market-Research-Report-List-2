<p><p>Stage monitors are essential audio devices used in live performances to provide sound reinforcement and monitoring for performers on stage. Active monitors have built-in power amplifiers and require only a sound source to function. They offer convenience and flexibility, as they can be easily connected to any sound system. Passive monitors, on the other hand, do not have built-in amplifiers and require an external power amplifier to operate. They offer higher sound quality and are commonly used in professional settings where more power and control are needed.</p></p>
