<p><p>Cardiac Medical Devices market consists of various types such as Cardiac Rhythm Management (CRM) Devices, Cardiac Assist Devices, Heart Valve, and Others. CRM Devices includes pacemakers and defibrillators used to monitor and regulate heart rhythms. Cardiac Assist Devices are devices that aid the heart's pumping function, such as ventricular assist devices and intra-aortic balloon pumps. Heart Valve devices are used to repair or replace damaged heart valves. The Others category encompasses devices like implantable loop recorders and interventional cardiology devices used in cardiac procedures. These devices play a vital role in the diagnosis and treatment of cardiac conditions.</p></p>
