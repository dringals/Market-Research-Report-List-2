<p><ul><li>On-premises</li><li>Cloud-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Investment management software can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed and operated on the user's local hardware and infrastructure. It provides a high level of control and security but requires ongoing maintenance and updates. On the other hand, cloud-based software is accessible via the internet and hosted on remote servers. It offers scalability, flexibility, and automatic updates, with the data stored securely in the cloud. This allows users to access and manage their investments from anywhere, anytime, while reducing the need for extensive IT infrastructure.</p></p>
