<p><p>Rigging screws are used in various applications to adjust or tension wires, cables, or ropes. There are two main types of rigging screws in the market: open-body and closed-body. Open-body rigging screws have a threaded turnbuckle-like design that allows for easy adjustment and tensioning. They are commonly used in applications where frequent adjustments are necessary. On the other hand, closed-body rigging screws feature a tubular design with a threaded insert that provides a more secure and streamlined solution. They are often chosen for applications requiring higher load capacities and increased safety.</p></p>
