<p><p>The contour brush market consists of different types, including facial contour brushes and nose shadow brushes. Facial contour brushes are designed specifically for contouring and highlighting the face. They typically have a rounded or angled shape and dense bristles for precise application and blending of makeup products. On the other hand, nose shadow brushes are smaller and more tapered, allowing for precise application of eyeshadow or contour products on the nose area. Both types of brushes cater to specific areas of the face, providing the necessary tools for achieving a flawlessly contoured look.</p></p>
