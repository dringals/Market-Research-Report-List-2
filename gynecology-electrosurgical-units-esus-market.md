<p><p>Gynecology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) include different types based on their functionality. Monopole ESUs are used for cutting and coagulating tissues with a single electrode, while Bipolar ESUs use two electrodes to safely seal smaller blood vessels. Vessel Sealing Units are designed specifically to seal larger blood vessels during gynecological procedures. Each type of ESU offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the complexity and requirements of the surgery being performed.</p></p>
