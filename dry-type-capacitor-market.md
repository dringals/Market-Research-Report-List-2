<p><p>Dry-type capacitors are categorized into three main types based on their filling materials: solid filling, semi-solid filling, and gas filling. Solid filling capacitors have their insulating material in a solid state, providing high insulation resistance and stability. Semi-solid filling capacitors use a combination of solid and liquid materials, offering a balance between stability and flexibility. Gas filling capacitors utilize a gas as the insulating material, providing excellent thermal conductivity and overload protection. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the market.</p></p>
