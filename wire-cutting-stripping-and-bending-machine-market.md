<p><p>The Wire Cutting Stripping and Bending Machine market is classified into two types: Semi-automatic and Automatic. These machines are suitable for smaller production volumes and offer cost-effective solutions. On the other hand, Automatic machines perform all the processes automatically, including wire loading and operation initiation.
