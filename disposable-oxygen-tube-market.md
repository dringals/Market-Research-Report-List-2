<p><p>The disposable oxygen tube market includes two types: Into The Water Humidification Tube and Surface Humidification Tube. Into The Water Humidification Tube is used to deliver oxygen to patients by bubbling it through water, increasing humidity and making it easier to breathe. Surface Humidification Tube, on the other hand, does not require water and delivers humidified oxygen directly to the patient's airway. Both types offer convenient and hygienic options for patients who require supplementary oxygen for various medical conditions.</p></p>
