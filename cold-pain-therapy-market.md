<p><p>Cold pain therapy products can be categorized into two types: OTC (Over-The-Counter) products and prescription products. OTC products are easily accessible and do not require a doctor's prescription. These products typically include cold packs, wraps, gels, sprays, and patches. On the other hand, prescription products require a doctor's prescription and are usually more potent and specialized. These may include prescription-strength cold packs, creams, or medications.
