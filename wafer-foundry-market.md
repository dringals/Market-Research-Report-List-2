<p><strong>The Wafer Foundry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LiTaO3</li><li>Quartz</li><li>LiNbO3</li><li>Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer foundry market consists of different types, including LiTaO3, Quartz, LiNbO3, and Bonded wafers. LiTaO3 wafers are made of lithium tantalate and are commonly used in applications like surface acoustic wave (SAW) devices. Quartz wafers are made of quartz crystal and are used in various electronic applications like sensors and timing devices. LiNbO3 wafers, made of lithium niobate, find applications in optoelectronics and photonics. Bonded wafers are created by bonding two or more wafers together, offering enhanced mechanical strength and electrical performance for specific applications.</p></p>
