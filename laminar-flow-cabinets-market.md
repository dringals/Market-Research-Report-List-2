<p><p>Laminar flow cabinets are used to provide a controlled environment for processes that require a high level of cleanliness and sterility. Horizontal laminar flow cabinets have a fan system that pushes the air horizontally across the workspace, creating a clean airflow. Vertical laminar flow cabinets have a similar design, but the air flows vertically from the top of the cabinet. Both types of cabinets are used in various industries such as laboratories, pharmaceuticals, and electronics manufacturing to protect sensitive materials and equipment from contamination.</p></p>
