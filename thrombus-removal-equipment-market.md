<p><p>The Thrombus Removal Equipment market consists of various types of equipment used for removing blood clots. Hydrodynamic devices use high-pressure jets of fluid to break down the clot, while ultrasound devices employ sound waves to break up the clot. Aspiration devices use suction to extract the clot, and mechanical devices physically disrupt its structure. Other devices may include pharmaceutical or catheter-based methods. Each type has its own benefits and risks, and the choice depends on factors such as clot location and patient characteristics.</p></p>
