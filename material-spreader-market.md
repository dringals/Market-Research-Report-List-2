<p><p>The material spreader market consists of two main types: manual material spreaders and electric material spreaders. Manual material spreaders require the user to physically operate the spreader by pushing or cranking a handle, allowing for control over the spread of materials such as seeds or fertilizer. On the other hand, electric material spreaders are powered by electricity and often feature automated settings for more precise material distribution. Both types serve the purpose of evenly dispersing materials over a given area, but electric spreaders offer the convenience of automation.</p></p>
