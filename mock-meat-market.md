<p><p>Mock meat, also known as meat analogs or meat substitutes, refers to plant-based or lab-grown alternatives to traditional animal meat products. Mock meat serves as a sustainable and cruelty-free alternative for individuals who follow vegetarian, vegan, or flexitarian diets, or for those looking to reduce their consumption of animal products.</p><p>The global mock meat market has witnessed substantial growth in recent years and is expected to continue to expand at a steady pace.
