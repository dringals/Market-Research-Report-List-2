<p><p>Silicon photonics products refer to optical components and devices that utilize the properties of silicon to manipulate and transmit light signals. Optical cables are fiber-optic cables used to transmit data over long distances using light signals. Transceivers are integrated devices that transmit and receive optical signals, typically used in communication systems. Optical multiplexers combine multiple optical signals into a single signal for efficient transmission. Optical attenuators are used to reduce the power of optical signals. Other silicon photonics products include switches, modulators, photodetectors, and wavelength division multiplexers, which enable various functionalities in optical communication systems.</p></p>
