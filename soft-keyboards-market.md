<p><p>A soft keyboard (also known as a virtual keyboard or on-screen keyboard) is a digital input device that replaces the traditional physical keyboard. Soft keyboards are widely used in smartphones, tablets, gaming consoles, kiosks, and other portable electronic devices.</p><p>The global soft keyboards market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing adoption of smartphones and tablets, coupled with the growing need for compact and portable input devices, is driving the market's growth.
