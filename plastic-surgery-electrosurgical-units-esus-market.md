<p><p>Plastic Surgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) market includes Monopole Electrosurgical Units, Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, and Vessel Sealing Units. Monopole ESUs use a single electrode for cutting and coagulation, while Bipolar ESUs use two electrodes to ensure precise tissue sealing. Vessel Sealing Units are specifically designed to seal blood vessels during surgery, reducing bleeding and improving patient outcomes. Each type of ESU caters to different surgical needs, offering a wide range of options for plastic surgeons to choose from.</p></p>
