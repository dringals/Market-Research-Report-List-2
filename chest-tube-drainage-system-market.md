<p><p>The Chest Tube Drainage System market consists of two types: Single Chamber and Multiple Chamber. A Single Chamber system includes a single container that collects and stores fluid from the chest cavity. It is commonly used for simple cases of fluid drainage. On the other hand, a Multiple Chamber system consists of two or more interconnected containers that separate air and fluid. This type of system is typically used for more complex cases, as it allows for better control and monitoring of chest cavity conditions.</p></p>
