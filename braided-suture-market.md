<p><strong>The Braided Suture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Braided Suture</li><li>Non-absorbable Braided Suture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The braided suture market is divided into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable braided sutures. Absorbable braided sutures are made from materials that break down over time and are absorbed by the body, eliminating the need for removal. These sutures are commonly used in internal surgeries. On the other hand, non-absorbable braided sutures are made from materials that are not absorbed by the body and require manual removal after healing. These sutures are usually used in external surgeries where long-term support is needed.</p></p>
