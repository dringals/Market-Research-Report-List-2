<p><p>Vials and Ampoules Labelling Machines come in three main types - Rotary, Horizontal, and Vertical. Rotary machines are designed for high-speed applications, handling multiple containers simultaneously. Horizontal machines label containers that pass horizontally through the machine, making them suitable for larger-sized vials and ampoules. Vertical machines label containers that move vertically through the machine, optimizing space and providing flexibility in labeling different sizes of containers. Each type of machine caters to different production requirements and container sizes in the pharmaceutical and medical industries.</p></p>
