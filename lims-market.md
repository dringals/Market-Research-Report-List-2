<p><strong>The LIMS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-premise LIMS</li><li>Cloud-based LIMS</li><li>Remotely Hosted LIMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LIMS, or Laboratory Information Management System, consists of three main market types: on-premise LIMS, cloud-based LIMS, and remotely hosted LIMS. On-premise LIMS refers to systems that are installed and operated locally on the laboratory's own servers and infrastructure. Cloud-based LIMS, on the other hand, is accessed and operated through the internet, allowing for remote access, data storage, and system updates. Remotely hosted LIMS involve the storage and management of data on external servers, typically belonging to a third-party provider, while the laboratory accesses the system remotely.</p></p>
