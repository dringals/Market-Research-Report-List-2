<p><p>The Bluetooth Chipset Market consists of two main types: Single Mode and Dual Mode. Single Mode chipsets are designed for low-power applications that require basic Bluetooth functionality, typically found in wearable devices, sensors, and healthcare monitors. On the other hand, Dual Mode chipsets provide both Classic Bluetooth and Bluetooth Low Energy support, enabling connectivity with a wider range of devices like smartphones, laptops, and audio peripherals. This flexibility makes Dual Mode chipsets suitable for various applications, including IoT devices, automotive systems, and home automation solutions.</p></p>
