<p><p>Hydraulic Service Manifolds (HSMs) are used in hydraulic systems to control and distribute fluid flow. There are two main types of HSMs: modular manifolds and single-piece manifolds. Modular manifolds consist of separate components that can be assembled in different configurations, offering flexibility and customization. Single-piece manifolds, on the other hand, are pre-assembled and cannot be modified. Both types cater to different market needs, with modular manifolds suitable for complex systems requiring flexibility, while single-piece manifolds are preferred for simpler, standard applications.</p></p>
