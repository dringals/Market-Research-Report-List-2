<p><p>Air balloon burners are devices that provide the necessary heat for hot air balloons to elevate. These burners generally use liquid propane gas as the fuel source and consist of a burner head, a gas control system, and a fuel tank. The burner head includes multiple jets that spray the gas, which is then ignited to produce a controlled flame. This flame heats the air inside the balloon envelope, causing it to rise and lift the balloon off the ground.</p><p>The global air balloon burners market is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
