<p><p>The high-end accelerometer market is classified into three types: 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis. </p><p>A 1-axis accelerometer measures acceleration in a single direction. It is suitable for applications such as tilt sensing or vibration monitoring along one axis only. </p><p>A 2-axis accelerometer measures acceleration in two perpendicular directions. It is commonly used in applications requiring tilt sensing or orientation detection along two axes, such as gaming consoles or drones. </p><p>A 3-axis accelerometer measures acceleration in three perpendicular directions. It is versatile and commonly used in applications requiring precise motion sensing or orientation detection along all three axes, like smartphones, wearables, or navigation systems.</p></p>
