<p><p>The ophthalmic slit lamp microscope market can be categorized into two main types: desktop slit lamp and portable slit lamp. Desktop slit lamps are fixed units typically found in clinics and hospitals. They offer higher magnification and advanced features, allowing for detailed eye examinations. On the other hand, portable slit lamps are compact and lightweight, designed for on-the-go ophthalmic exams. They are convenient for remote or rural areas, enabling healthcare professionals to provide eye care services outside traditional medical settings. Both types play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating various eye conditions.</p></p>
