<p><strong>The On-Demand Food Delivery Platforms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Restaurant-to-Consumer Delivery</li><li>Platform-to-Consumer Delivery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-Demand Food Delivery Platforms are divided into two major market types: Restaurant-to-Consumer Delivery and Platform-to-Consumer Delivery (also known as Aggregator Delivery). In Restaurant-to-Consumer Delivery, platforms work as intermediaries between consumers and restaurants, enabling customers to order food directly from their preferred restaurants. The platform then arranges for the delivery of the ordered food. In Platform-to-Consumer Delivery, however, platforms partner with various restaurants and offer a wide range of food options to consumers on their app or website. The platform handles the delivery by utilizing their own logistics network or working with third-party delivery partners.</p></p>
