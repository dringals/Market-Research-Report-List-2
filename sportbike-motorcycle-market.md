<p><p>A sportbike motorcycle, also known as a sports bike or a superbike, is a high-performance motorcycle designed for speed, acceleration, and cornering on paved roads. Sportbikes are popular among motorcycle enthusiasts who enjoy the thrill of high-speed riding and participating in sports bike racing.</p><p>The sportbike motorcycle market has been experiencing steady growth over the years. Factors driving the market growth include the increasing popularity of motorcycle racing events, technological advancements in motorcycle engineering, and rising disposable incomes of consumers in developing countries. Manufacturers are incorporating features like anti-lock braking systems (ABS), traction control, quick-shifters, and electronic suspension systems to enhance rider safety and improve overall riding experience. These advancements not only make sportbikes safer but also attract a wider customer base, including riders who prioritize safety and comfort.</p><p>Furthermore, the market has witnessed a rise in the customization trend, with riders tailoring their sportbikes according to their preferences and personal style.
