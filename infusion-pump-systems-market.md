<p><p>Infusion pump systems are medical devices used to deliver fluids into a patient's body in a controlled manner. There are several types of infusion pump systems available in the market. Volumetric infusion pump systems deliver fluids in predetermined volumes, while syringe infusion pump systems deliver fluids through syringes. Ambulatory infusion pump systems are portable and can be carried by patients. Enteral infusion pump systems are used for delivering nutrients directly into the gastrointestinal tract. Insulin infusion pump systems are specifically designed for delivering insulin. Implantable infusion pump systems are surgically implanted and provide continuous medication delivery. Anesthesia infusion pump systems deliver anesthesia medications during surgeries. Chemotherapy infusion pump systems administer chemotherapy drugs to cancer patients.</p></p>
