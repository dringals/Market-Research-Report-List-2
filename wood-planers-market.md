<p><p>Wood planers are used to smooth and flatten rough wood surfaces. There are three main market types: manual planers, electric planers, and stationary planers. Manual planers are operated by hand and are suitable for small woodworking tasks. Electric planers are powered by electricity and offer greater efficiency and precision. They are versatile and can handle various wood thicknesses. Stationary planers, as the name suggests, are fixed machines commonly used in professional woodworking settings. They are larger in size, stable, and provide consistent results for commercial projects.</p></p>
