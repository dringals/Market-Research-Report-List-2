<p><p>The photoresistor market consists of three main types: UV photoresistors, infrared photoresistors, and visible light photoresistors. UV photoresistors are designed to detect ultraviolet light, commonly used in various applications such as sterilization, water purification, and flame detection. Infrared photoresistors are sensitive to infrared light, enabling them to be used in night vision cameras, security systems, and remote controls. Visible light photoresistors are specifically engineered to detect visible light and are commonly employed in light-sensitive applications like streetlights, photography, and automatic lighting controls.</p></p>
