Additionally, the technological advancements in endoscopic devices and the growing adoption of robotic-assisted surgeries are driving market growth.</p><p>One of the key trends in the market is the shift towards disposable fluid management systems. Disposable systems offer various advantages such as reduced risk of infection, improved patient safety, and cost-effectiveness.
