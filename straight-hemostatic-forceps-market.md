<p><p>The Straight Hemostatic Forceps market comprises three main types: Halstead Mosquito Hemostatic Forceps, Kelly and Crile Hemostatic Forceps, and Rochester-Carmalt Hemostatic Forceps. Halstead Mosquito Hemostatic Forceps are small, delicate forceps used to grasp small blood vessels during surgeries. Kelly and Crile Hemostatic Forceps are medium-sized forceps with serrated jaws for clamping larger vessels. Rochester-Carmalt Hemostatic Forceps are large forceps with cross-serrated jaws, commonly used for clamping and securing larger vessels or tissue bundles. These different types cater to the varying needs and sizes of blood vessel clamping during surgical procedures.</p></p>
