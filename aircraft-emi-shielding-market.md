<p><p>The Aircraft EMI Shielding market offers various types of shielding solutions. Gaskets are used to provide a seal while blocking electromagnetic interference (EMI). Cable overbraids are wire coverings that protect cables from external interference. Laminates include adhesive layers that shield aircraft components. Tapes and foils are applied to specific areas to prevent EMI. Conductive coatings and paints are used to create a shielding layer on aircraft surfaces. Lastly, there are other EMI shielding options available that may include products such as filters or ferrites. These different types of shielding solutions help secure aircraft systems from electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
