<p><p>Proximity as a Service (PaaS) markets can be categorized into different types based on the technology used for proximity communication. Wi-Fi-based PaaS enables devices to connect wirelessly within a specific range, offering high-speed data transfers. Bluetooth-based PaaS allows short-range connections between devices for file sharing, audio streaming, etc. Near Field Communication (NFC) enables contactless communication between devices within a close range, commonly used for mobile payments. GPS-based PaaS utilizes satellite positioning for location-based services. Other types may include infrared, ultrasound, or Zigbee for proximity-based communication and services.</p></p>
