<p><strong>The Building Mechanical Ventilation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation</li><li>Supply Ventilation</li><li>Balanced Ventilation</li><li>Energy Recovery Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Building mechanical ventilation systems are used to ensure proper airflow within buildings for comfort and health purposes. Exhaust ventilation type removes stale air from indoors, while supply ventilation type brings in fresh outdoor air. Balanced ventilation combines both exhaust and supply systems to maintain a balanced airflow. Energy recovery ventilation uses heat exchange technology to recover energy from exhaust air and pre-condition incoming fresh air. These systems are crucial in ensuring optimal air quality and energy efficiency in buildings.</p></p>
