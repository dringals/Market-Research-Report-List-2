<p><p>Cabinet drawer runners come in three main types: Wooden runners, Roller runners, and Ball Bearing runners. Wooden runners are traditional and cost-effective options that are often seen in older cabinets. Roller runners are more modern and provide smoother operation by allowing the drawer to glide on rollers. Ball Bearing runners are the most expensive but offer the smoothest and most durable operation due to the use of ball bearings. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
