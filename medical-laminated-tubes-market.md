<p><strong>The Medical Laminated Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical laminated tubes refer to packaging tubes used in the healthcare industry for storing and dispensing various medical products such as ointments, creams, gels, and pharmaceuticals. ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminate) tubes are made of aluminum laminated with plastic layers, providing excellent barrier properties against moisture and light. PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate) tubes, on the other hand, are made entirely of plastic layers, offering flexibility and compatibility with a wide range of formulations. Both ABL and PBL tubes are widely used in the medical sector due to their protective and convenient features.</p></p>
