<p><p>Water Saving Integrated Toilets have two main market types: Flush Type and Siphon Type. Flush Type toilets use a mechanism that allows water to flow quickly and forcefully into the bowl, effectively removing waste. Siphon Type toilets, on the other hand, create a vacuum that pulls waste from the bowl and into the plumbing system. Both types of toilets are designed to minimize water consumption while maintaining efficient flushing capabilities, making them eco-friendly options for water conservation in households and commercial settings.</p></p>
