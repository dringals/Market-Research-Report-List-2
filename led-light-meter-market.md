<p><p>The LED Light Meter Market can be classified into three types based on their measurement range: Short, Medium, and Wide. The Short Measurement Range meters are designed to measure light intensity in close proximity to the source, such as laboratory or room testing. Medium Measurement Range meters offer the capability to measure light intensity within a moderate distance, suitable for indoor lighting applications. Wide Measurement Range meters have a higher capacity to measure light intensity over long distances and are commonly used for outdoor lighting or large-scale industrial environments.</p></p>
