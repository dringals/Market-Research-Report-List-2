<p><strong>The Foot Orthoses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Orthotics</li><li>Static Orthotics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foot orthoses are devices designed to support and align the foot while walking or running. Dynamic orthotics are flexible inserts that provide both support and cushioning, allowing for natural foot movement. They are suitable for people with conditions such as plantar fasciitis or Achilles tendonitis. Static orthotics, on the other hand, are rigid and provide stability for individuals with foot deformities or excessive pronation. They help correct foot imbalances and improve posture. Both types of orthotics are commonly used to alleviate foot pain and improve overall foot function.</p></p>
