<p><p>The steam ejector market comprises two types: single-stage steam ejectors and multi-stage steam ejectors. Single-stage steam ejectors are commonly used for low vacuum applications. They have a simple design with one set of nozzles to compress and evacuate the desired gas or vapor. On the other hand, multi-stage steam ejectors are suitable for high vacuum applications. They consist of multiple stages of nozzles, each stage condensing and compressing the steam further to achieve a higher vacuum level. Both types play a crucial role in various industries requiring vacuum operations.</p></p>
