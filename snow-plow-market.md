<p><p>In the snow plow market, two common types are straight blades and V-plows. Straight blades have a single, flat blade that clears snow in a straight line. They are versatile and effective on most surfaces but lack maneuverability. V-plows have a V-shaped configuration with two angled blades that can independently or simultaneously move to push snow to the sides. They are ideal for clearing large areas and offer better maneuverability around obstacles. Both types have their advantages and are popular choices in the snow plow market.</p></p>
