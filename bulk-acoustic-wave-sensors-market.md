<p><p>The Bulk Acoustic Wave Sensor market is comprised of two main types: QCM-TSM and FBAR-SMR. QCM-TSM (Quartz Crystal Microbalance-Thin Film Thickness Sensor) utilizes a quartz crystal to measure changes in mass, viscosity, and other properties of thin films. FBAR-SMR (Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator-Surface Membrane Resonator) uses thin film resonators to detect changes in frequency caused by external factors. Both types are widely used in various industries, including automotive, healthcare, and defense, for applications such as gas sensing, chemical analysis, and wireless communication.</p></p>
