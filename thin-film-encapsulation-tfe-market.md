<p><p>Thin-Film Encapsulation (TFE) market includes two types of encapsulation methods - Organic Layers and Inorganic Layers. Organic Layers use organic materials such as polymers to protect the device from external elements, while Inorganic Layers use inorganic materials like ceramics for encapsulation. Both types offer different levels of protection and barrier properties for various applications. Organic layers are commonly used in flexible displays and organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs), while inorganic layers are preferred for higher durability and improved moisture resistance in applications like integrated circuits.</p></p>
