<p><p>Foodborne pathogen test kits are used to detect harmful microorganisms in food samples. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) test kits use a method to amplify and detect specific DNA sequences of pathogens, providing accurate and rapid results. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) test kits detect specific proteins or antibodies associated with foodborne pathogens. Both PCR and ELISA test kits are widely used in the food industry to ensure food safety, prevent outbreaks, and comply with regulatory standards, offering different approaches for detecting and identifying potential hazards in food products.</p></p>
