Measurement Systems consist of sensors and software that monitor various parameters like milk yield and cow health, aiding in farm management decisions. Cooling Systems are responsible for cooling the milk immediately after milking to maintain its quality. These three types of systems together facilitate a more streamlined and productive milking process for dairy farmers.</p></p>
