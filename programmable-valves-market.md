<p><p>Programmable valves are a type of valve that can be adjusted or controlled electronically. There are two main types of programmable valves: magnetic adjustment and gravity adjustment valves. Magnetic adjustment valves use electromagnetic forces to adjust the flow of fluids or gases through the valve. Gravity adjustment valves, on the other hand, use the force of gravity to control the valve's opening and closing. Both types of valves offer precise and programmable control over the flow, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
