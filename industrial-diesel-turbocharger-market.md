<p><p>The Industrial Diesel Turbocharger market is divided into two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. OEM refers to turbochargers that are produced and sold by the original equipment manufacturers, which are typically installed in diesel engines during the manufacturing process. Aftermarket turbochargers, on the other hand, are sold separately from the original equipment manufacturers and are often chosen by consumers as replacements or upgrades for their existing diesel engines. Both types cater to the demand for turbochargers in the industrial diesel sector but differ in terms of production source and timing of installation.</p></p>
