<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT) Flooring market can be categorized into two main types - rigid and flexible. Rigid LVT flooring is constructed using a solid core made of materials like composite, stone polymer, or wood plastic. It offers enhanced stability, durability, and resistance to moisture. On the other hand, flexible LVT flooring is made with a flexible core, allowing the tiles to bend slightly. This type is more suitable for uneven surfaces and areas with more foot traffic. Both types provide a wide range of design options and are popular choices in the market.</p></p>
