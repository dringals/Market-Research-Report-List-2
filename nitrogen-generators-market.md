<p><strong>The Nitrogen Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Cryogenic Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrogen generators are devices that produce nitrogen gas from compressed air. There are three main types of nitrogen generators on the market today: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Membrane, and Cryogenic Air. PSA nitrogen generators use carbon molecular sieves to separate nitrogen from other gases. Membrane nitrogen generators utilize hollow fiber membranes to separate nitrogen from oxygen. Cryogenic air generators use a process of chilling air to very low temperatures to separate out nitrogen gas. Each type of generator has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
