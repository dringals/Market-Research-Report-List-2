<p><p>Inflatable packers are a type of equipment used in various industries for sealing off sections of wellbores during drilling, completion, and production operations. They come in different types based on their design and functionality. Single inflatable packers consist of one single inflatable element that can be expanded or deflated to seal a specific area. Double inflatable packers, on the other hand, contain two separate inflatable elements for improved isolation and sealing. The "Others" market category refers to any other types of inflatable packers available in the market, such as triple inflatable packers or specialty packers designed for specific applications.</p></p>
