<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Spray Booth Ventilation System market consists of three main types: Side Draft, Cross Draft, and Down Draft. Side Draft systems pull air horizontally across the work area, creating a continuous and consistent airflow. Cross Draft systems draw air from one side and exhaust it on the opposite side, ensuring even coverage. Down Draft systems pull air from the top of the booth and expel it through the floor, eliminating airborne contaminants effectively. Each type has its advantages and is used in various industries, depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
