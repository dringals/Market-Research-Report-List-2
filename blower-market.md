<p><p>The blower market can be classified into three types based on pressure levels: low pressure, medium voltage, and high pressure. Low pressure blowers are designed for applications that require a small amount of airflow at low pressures, such as ventilation systems in residential buildings or small-scale industrial processes. Medium voltage blowers are capable of generating moderate airflow at medium pressures, commonly used in larger industrial applications like material handling or pneumatic conveying. High-pressure blowers, on the other hand, are built for heavy-duty applications requiring high airflow at high pressures, like air pollution control systems or power plants.</p></p>
