<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) System market encompasses various types of techniques used for thin film deposition. Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition involves the use of high-energy electric arcs to vaporize coating materials. Electron Beam Vapor Deposition utilizes an electron beam to vaporize and deposit materials onto a substrate. Sputter Deposition involves ion bombardment of a target material to create a vapor that condenses onto the substrate. Ion Implantation implants ions into a substrate to alter its properties. Pulsed Laser Vapor Deposition employs laser pulses to create a plasma and deposit materials onto the substrate.</p></p>
