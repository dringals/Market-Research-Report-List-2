<p><p>The airport supply chain market can be categorized into two types: internal supply chain and external supply chain markets. The internal supply chain refers to the processes and activities involved within the airport, including procurement, inventory management, and logistics. On the other hand, the external supply chain market involves the interactions between the airport and external entities, such as airlines, cargo operators, and suppliers. This encompasses activities like transportation, warehousing, and distribution to ensure a smooth flow of goods and services both within and outside the airport premises.</p></p>
