<p><p>The Smart Upper Limb Exoskeleton Market is divided into two types: Soft Exoskeleton and Rigid Exoskeleton. Soft Exoskeletons are made of flexible materials and provide external support to the limbs while allowing natural movement. They are lightweight and more comfortable to wear, making them suitable for long-term use. On the other hand, Rigid Exoskeletons are constructed with rigid materials and offer greater stability and strength. Although they may be less comfortable, they provide better support for individuals with severe limb impairments. Both types aim to enhance mobility and functionality in individuals with upper limb disabilities.</p></p>
