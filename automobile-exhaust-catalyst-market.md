<p><p>Automobile exhaust catalyst market types include Three-way Catalyst (TWC), Four-Way Catalyst (4WC), Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC), Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Catalyst, Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Catalyst, Ammonia Slip Catalyst (ASC), and others. TWCs are used in gasoline engines to reduce emissions of carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides, and hydrocarbons. DOCs are used in diesel engines to oxidize carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons. SCR catalysts reduce nitrogen oxide emissions in diesel engines. DPF catalysts trap and remove particulate matter from diesel engine exhaust. ASCs are used to reduce ammonia emissions in SCR systems.</p></p>
