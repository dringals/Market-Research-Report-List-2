<p><p>Steam flowmeters are instruments used to measure the flow rate of steam in a system. There are several types of steam flowmeters available in the market. Orifice flowmeters work by measuring the pressure drop across an orifice plate. Turbine flowmeters use a spinning rotor to measure the steam flow. Ultrasonic flowmeters calculate the flow rate by measuring the time it takes for sound waves to travel through the steam. Vortex flowmeters measure the frequency of vortices formed when steam flows past an obstruction. Other types of steam flowmeters include mass and thermal flowmeters.</p></p>
