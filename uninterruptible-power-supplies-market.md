<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uninterruptible Power Supplies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Off-line or standby</li><li>Line-interactive</li><li>Online or double-conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) market consists of three major types. The first type is Off-line or standby UPS, where power is normally supplied directly from the mains, but switches to battery backup in case of power outage. The second type is Line-interactive UPS, which constantly regulates and stabilizes voltage levels, using battery backup only when necessary. Lastly, Online or double-conversion UPS provides continuous battery power, converting AC power to DC and back to AC, ensuring uninterrupted supply regardless of the main power source. These types cater to different needs and environments.</p></p>
