<p><strong>The Mercury Testing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Mercury Analysis</li><li>Trace Mercury analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mercury Testing Service market can be categorized into two main types: Total Mercury Analysis and Trace Mercury Analysis. Total Mercury Analysis involves analyzing samples to determine the total amount of mercury present, without specifying its various forms. This type of analysis is commonly used in environmental monitoring and compliance purposes. On the other hand, Trace Mercury Analysis focuses on quantifying very low levels of mercury, often in the parts per billion (ppb) range. This type of analysis is typically employed in industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing, where even small traces of mercury need to be detected and controlled.</p></p>
