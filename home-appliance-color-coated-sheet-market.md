<p><p>Home Appliance Color-Coated Sheet market includes PCM (Pre-Coated Metal) and VCM (Vinyl Coated Metal) types. PCM sheets are coated with a layer of organic paint that offers durability, scratch resistance, and vibrant color options for appliances. On the other hand, VCM sheets have a layer of vinyl film that provides a glossy finish, easy cleaning, and aesthetic appeal to home appliances. Both PCM and VCM sheets are widely used in the manufacturing of refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, and other household appliances.</p></p>
