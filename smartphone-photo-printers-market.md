<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Smartphone photo printers are available in two main types: thermal dye sublimation printers and inkjet printers. Thermal dye sublimation printers work by transferring solid dye from a ribbon onto the printing paper using heat, resulting in high-quality, water-resistant prints. Inkjet printers, on the other hand, use liquid ink sprayed onto the paper to create prints. Both types offer convenience and portability, allowing users to print directly from their smartphones. However, thermal dye sublimation printers generally produce better image quality, while inkjet printers offer more flexibility in terms of paper types and sizes.</p></p>
