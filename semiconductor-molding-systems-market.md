<p><p>Semiconductor molding systems are devices used in the manufacturing process of semiconductors. These systems are available in three market types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic systems are highly automated and require minimal human intervention. They offer high efficiency and productivity. Semi-automatic systems involve a combination of automated and manual processes, requiring moderate human intervention. Manual systems, on the other hand, rely primarily on manual labor and involve less automation. Each market type offers different levels of automation and is suitable for various manufacturing needs and budgets.</p></p>
