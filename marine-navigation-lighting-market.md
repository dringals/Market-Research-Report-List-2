<p><p>Marine navigation lighting includes various types of lights that are essential for ensuring safe navigation at sea. Side lights are located on the sides of a vessel and help indicate its direction. All-round lights are positioned to provide visibility from all angles. Stern lights are located at the rear of a vessel, while masthead lights are positioned at the highest point to indicate the presence of a vessel from a distance. Each type of light serves a specific purpose in guiding maritime traffic and preventing collisions.</p></p>
