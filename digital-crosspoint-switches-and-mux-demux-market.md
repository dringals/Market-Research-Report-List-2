<p><p>Digital crosspoint switches and mux & demux are essential components used in communication systems to manage and direct the flow of digital signals. </p><p>A digital crosspoint switch is a device that connects multiple input signals to multiple output signals in a non-blocking manner. It allows the selection and routing of any input signal to any output, providing flexibility and efficient signal management in various applications such as telecommunications, data centers, and broadcasting.</p><p>Mux & demux, on the other hand, are modules used to combine multiple input signals onto a single signal (multiplexing) or separate a single signal into multiple output signals (demultiplexing). They are commonly used in optical fiber networks to maximize bandwidth utilization.</p><p>The market for digital crosspoint switches and mux & demux is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period.
