<p><p>High-resolution industrial inkjet printers are classified into two main market types: handheld type and fixed type. Handheld inkjet printers are portable and can be easily carried and operated by hand. They offer flexibility and are suitable for marking objects that are difficult to move. On the other hand, fixed inkjet printers are stationary and integrated into production lines or printing stations. They are designed for continuous high-volume printing and are used for marking, coding, and labeling on various surfaces such as packaging materials and industrial products.</p></p>
