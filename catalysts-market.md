There are four main types of catalysts: zeolites, enzymes, chemical compounds, and metals. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates with a porous structure that facilitate chemical reactions. Enzymes are biological catalysts that speed up reactions in living organisms. Chemical compounds, such as oxides and sulfides, are used in a wide range of industrial processes. Metals like platinum, palladium, and nickel are commonly used catalysts in refining and hydrogenation reactions.</p></p>
