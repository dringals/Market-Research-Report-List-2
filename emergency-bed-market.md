<p><p>The emergency bed market is diversified into three main types: electric, manual, and hydraulic beds. Electric beds are equipped with motorized systems that allow patients to adjust the position of the bed effortlessly. Manual beds, on the other hand, require manual effort to adjust the bed's position, making them more cost-effective. Hydraulic beds function using hydraulic systems, offering smooth and controlled movements for patients. These different bed types cater to the varying needs and preferences of hospitals, healthcare facilities, and individuals seeking emergency care.</p></p>
