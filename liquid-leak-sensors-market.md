<p><strong>The Liquid Leak Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive Leak Detection</li><li>Non-Invasive Leak Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid leak sensors are used to detect the presence of leaks in liquid systems and prevent potential damage. Invasive leak detection involves sensors that are directly installed into the liquid-carrying pipes or containers and can detect leaks by measuring changes in pressure or conductivity. Non-invasive leak detection, on the other hand, utilizes external sensors that do not require physical contact with the liquid system to detect leaks. These sensors use techniques such as ultrasound, infrared, or electromagnetic waves to identify leaks by analyzing changes in the surrounding environment.</p></p>
