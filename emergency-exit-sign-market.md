<p><p>Emergency exit signs are crucial for ensuring the safety of individuals during emergencies. Two main types of emergency exit signs are the electrical exit sign and the non-electrical exit sign. Electrical exit signs use electricity for illumination and are usually equipped with backup power sources. These signs are beneficial in areas with low visibility or during power outages. On the other hand, non-electrical exit signs do not require electricity as they rely on alternative light sources, such as glow-in-the-dark materials. Non-electrical exit signs are cost-effective, environmentally friendly, and suitable for areas with steady lighting conditions.</p></p>
