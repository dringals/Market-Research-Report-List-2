<p><ul><li>Positioning Water Leakage Detector Systems</li><li>Non-positioning Water Leakage Detector Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water Leakage Detector Systems are used to detect leaks in water pipelines and prevent water damage. There are two main types of these systems - Positioning and Non-positioning. Positioning systems utilize sensors or detectors placed at specific points in the pipeline to identify the location of leaks accurately. Non-positioning systems, on the other hand, monitor overall water flow and pressure variations to detect leaks without pinpointing their exact location. Both types aim to minimize water wastage and potential property damage caused by leaks, providing efficient and cost-effective solutions for consumers.</p></p>
