<p><p>Backpack sprayers are available in two main market types based on their power source: manual and engine-powered. Manual backpack sprayers require the user to manually pump a lever to create pressure for spraying, making them ideal for smaller or more precise spraying tasks. On the other hand, engine-powered backpack sprayers are equipped with a motor that generates pressure for spraying, making them more suitable for larger areas and tougher spraying jobs. Both types offer their own set of advantages and are commonly used in agriculture, gardening, pest control, and landscaping.</p></p>
