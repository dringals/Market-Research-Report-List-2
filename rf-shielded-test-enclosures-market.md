<p><p>RF shielded test enclosures are used to create an electromagnetically isolated environment for conducting accurate and reliable measurements of electronic devices. The three main types of enclosures in the market are front, top, and others. Front shielding enclosures provide electromagnetic isolation from the front side, allowing testers to access the device easily. Top shielding enclosures offer isolation from the top side, enabling easy testing of devices placed inside. Other types may include enclosures that provide shielding from multiple sides or enclosures with specific features catering to unique testing requirements.</p></p>
