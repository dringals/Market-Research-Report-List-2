<p><strong>The Rolling Mill Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Rolling Mill</li><li>Cold Rolling Mill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rolling mill machine market can be broadly classified into two types: hot rolling mills and cold rolling mills. Hot rolling mills are used to shape materials by applying heat and pressure, resulting in the production of long metal sheets or bars. On the other hand, cold rolling mills operate at room temperature to refine and enhance the surface quality and thickness uniformity of metal sheets or coils through processes such as annealing and finish rolling. Both types of rolling mills play a crucial role in various industries, including automotive, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
