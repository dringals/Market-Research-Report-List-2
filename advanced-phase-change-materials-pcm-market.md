<p><p>Advanced Phase Change Materials (PCM) are materials that store and release energy by changing their phase, usually from solid to liquid and vice versa. There are three main types of PCM markets: organic, inorganic, and bio-based. Organic PCMs are derived from carbon-based compounds such as paraffin and esters. Inorganic PCMs are made from salts and metals like sodium sulfate and aluminum. Bio-based PCMs are derived from renewable sources such as vegetable oils and fatty acids. Each type of PCM has unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
