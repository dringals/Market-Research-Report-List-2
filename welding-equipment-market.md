<p><p>The welding equipment market is categorized into various types. Arc welding is a common type that uses an electric arc to create intense heat for joining materials. Electrogas/electroslag welding is a process using a consumable electrode to weld thick materials. Friction welding involves rubbing two materials together to generate friction-induced heat for fusion. Laser and electron-beam welding utilize lasers or electron beams to join materials. Resistance welding involves applying pressure and electric current to create a weld. Oxy-fuel welding uses a mixture of fuel gas and oxygen for welding. There are also other types of welding equipment available in the market.</p></p>
