<p><p>The Amateur Football Helmet Market can be categorized into two types based on the materials used: A) ABS material market, and B) TPU material market. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is a durable and impact-resistant thermoplastic, commonly used in football helmets due to its strength and cost-effectiveness. Both ABS and TPU helmets are popular choices among amateur football players, each with its own benefits and market share.</p></p>
