<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The VCM (Voice Coil Motor) Camera Driver IC market can be classified into three types: open loop VCM driver IC, closed loop VCM driver IC, and OIS (Optical Image Stabilization) VCM driver IC. Open loop VCM driver ICs provide a basic control mechanism without feedback for the movement of camera lenses, while closed loop VCM driver ICs use feedback to ensure accurate positioning. OIS VCM driver ICs specifically cater to image stabilization technology in cameras, reducing blurring caused by shaking or hand movements. Each of these types serves specific functions in camera modules.</p></p>
