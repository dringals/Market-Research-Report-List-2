<p><p>Hemodialysis is a medical treatment used for individuals with kidney failure. The market for hemodialysis machines includes three types: Hemodialysis Machine (HD), Hemodiafiltration Machine (HF), and Bedside Hemodiafiltration Machines (Bedside Machines). HD machines remove waste and excess fluid from the blood, HF machines combine hemodialysis with a filtration process, and Bedside Machines provide on-site blood filtration conveniently. These machines play a crucial role in the management of kidney failure, improving the overall quality of life for patients by effectively purifying their blood and maintaining appropriate fluid levels.</p></p>
