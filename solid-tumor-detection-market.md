<p><p>The solid tumor detection market consists of two types of testing: conventional testing and non-conventional testing. Conventional testing refers to the common methods used for tumor detection, such as imaging tests (CT scan, MRI), biopsies, and blood tests. Non-conventional testing refers to innovative and emerging techniques that aim to improve tumor detection, such as liquid biopsies (testing blood for tumor-specific markers), molecular profiling, and genetic testing. Both types of testing play a crucial role in diagnosing solid tumors and determining the most suitable treatment approach.</p></p>
