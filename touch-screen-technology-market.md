<p><p>The touch screen technology market can be categorized into various types. The first is resistive touchscreens, which use multiple layers that press against each other when touched. Capacitive touchscreens have a conductive layer that responds to the body's electrical charge. Infrared touchscreens use infrared light to detect touch. Surface acoustic wave type displays use ultrasonic waves to detect touch. Lastly, there are other types of touchscreens like optical imaging, projected capacitive, and acoustic pulse recognition, which each have their own unique features and functionalities.</p></p>
