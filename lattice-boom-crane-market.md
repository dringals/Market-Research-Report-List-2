<p><p>A Lattice Boom Crane is a type of mobile crane that consists of a lattice or truss boom, which is made up of a network of small, interconnected steel beams. This design provides the crane with strength and stability, making it ideal for heavy lifting applications in construction, oil and gas, and other industries.</p><p>The global Lattice Boom Crane Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.7% during the forecast period. Several factors are driving the growth of this market. Firstly, the demand for large-scale infrastructure projects is increasing worldwide, leading to a rise in construction activities. Lattice boom cranes are widely used in these projects for tasks such as lifting heavy materials, erecting steel structures, and demolishing old buildings.</p><p>Moreover, the oil and gas industry is also contributing to the market growth as lattice boom cranes are extensively used in offshore drilling operations to lift heavy loads and support drilling activities. The expanding renewable energy sector, particularly wind energy, is another key driver for the market. Lattice boom cranes are used for installation and maintenance of wind turbines, which are increasingly being deployed globally.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements and innovations in lattice boom cranes are propelling the market growth. Manufacturers are focusing on improving the efficiency, lifting capacity, and safety features of these cranes to meet the evolving requirements of end-users.</p><p>The market is witnessing some notable trends as well.
