<p><p>The portable lathe market can be classified into two types: vertical portable lathe and horizontal portable lathe. A vertical portable lathe is designed with a spindle that is positioned vertically, allowing for easy movement and maneuverability. This type of lathe is suitable for tasks that require precision and accuracy. On the other hand, a horizontal portable lathe has a spindle that is positioned horizontally, making it ideal for heavy-duty operations and tasks that involve turning large workpieces. Both types offer portability and convenience, catering to different needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
