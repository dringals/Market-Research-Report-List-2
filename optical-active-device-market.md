<p><p>The optical active device market encompasses various types of devices that play crucial roles in optical communication systems. Optical transceiver modules are devices that both transmit and receive optical signals, enabling high-speed data transfer over fiber-optic networks. Light detectors, on the other hand, convert optical signals into electrical signals, enabling their detection and processing. Light modulators are used to manipulate optical signals by modulating their intensity or phase. Additionally, there are other types of devices in the market that contribute to optical communication systems, ensuring efficient and reliable data transmission.</p></p>
