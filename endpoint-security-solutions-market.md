<p><p>Endpoint security solutions are classified into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based markets. On-premise solutions involve deploying security software and infrastructure within an organization's physical premises. This offers greater control and customization but requires extensive hardware and maintenance. Cloud-based solutions, on the other hand, rely on remote servers hosted by the solution provider. They offer scalability, accessibility, and reduced maintenance costs since the updates and management are handled by the provider. Organizations choose between these options based on their specific requirements and IT infrastructure preferences.</p></p>
