<p><p>The electric power cutter market can be classified into two main types: corded and battery-powered. On the other hand, battery-powered power cutters rely on rechargeable batteries for their operation. These cutters are portable and do not require a direct connection to a power source, making them suitable for outdoor and remote use. Both types offer their own advantages and considerations, depending on the specific needs and preferences of the users.</p></p>
