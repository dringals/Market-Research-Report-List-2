<p><p>Flood lighting refers to the illumination of a large outdoor area with high-intensity artificial light. <p><strong>The Flood Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Intensity Discharge(HID) Flood Lighting</li><li>LED Flood Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Intensity Discharge (HID) Flood Lighting is a type of flood lighting that uses high-intensity discharge lamps, such as metal halide or high-pressure sodium lamps. These lamps provide intense and bright illumination, making them suitable for large outdoor areas like stadiums, parking lots, and construction sites.</p><p>LED Flood Lighting is another type of flood lighting that utilizes Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) as the light source. LED flood lights are known for their energy efficiency, long lifespan, and low maintenance requirements. They are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings for various applications such as illuminating buildings, landscapes, and sports fields.</p></p>
