Traditional gauze dressings are being replaced by modern wound care dressings that offer better moisture management, breathability, and ease of use. These advanced dressings not only improve patient comfort but also enhance the healing process by creating an optimal wound environment.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the increasing adoption of antimicrobial gauze. Gauze tape, on the other hand, is a self-adhesive fabric strip that provides support and secures dressings in place. Gauze bandage is a stretchable fabric that is wrapped around the injured area to hold dressings or provide compression. These different types of gauze products cater to various needs in medical settings.</p></p>
