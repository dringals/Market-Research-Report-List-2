<p><p>The Anterior Cervical Fixation Devices market includes two types of products: Fixation Systems and Bone Grafts. Fixation Systems are used to stabilize the cervical spine, promoting fusion between the vertebrae. They usually consist of screws, plates, and rods that are surgically implanted to hold the cervical vertebrae in place. On the other hand, Bone Grafts are materials, such as autografts or allografts, that are placed between the vertebrae to stimulate bone growth and enhance fusion. Both Fixation Systems and Bone Grafts play crucial roles in the treatment of cervical spine disorders.</p></p>
