<p><p>Plasma lights, also known as plasma lamps or plasma globes, are lighting devices that consist of a glass bulb filled with a mixture of noble gases, as well as a high-frequency electromagnetic field generator. When activated, the gases inside the bulb ionize, resulting in the production of an electric plasma. This plasma emits a variety of vibrant colors and creates unique patterns that respond to touch or sound, making plasma lights popular as both decorative and entertainment lighting solutions.</p><p>The plasma lights market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years.
