<p><p>The regular rubber track market comprises two types: OEM and aftermarket. OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) tracks are produced by the original manufacturer of the vehicle or equipment. These tracks are specifically designed and tailored to fit the specific machine's requirements. On the other hand, aftermarket tracks are produced and sold by third-party manufacturers. These tracks serve as replacements for the original tracks and are made to fit a wide range of machines. Both OEM and aftermarket tracks cater to the needs of customers looking for durable and reliable rubber tracks for their vehicles or equipment.</p></p>
