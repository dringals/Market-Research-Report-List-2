<p><p>Honey powder is available in two market types: granulated honey and powdered honey. Granulated honey is made by dehydrating liquid honey and forming small granules, which can easily dissolve in liquids. It is suitable for applications like baking, dry mixes, and beverages. On the other hand, powdered honey is created by converting liquid honey into a fine powder. It is often used in dry spice blends, rubs, marinades, and as a topping for snacks. Both types offer convenience and a prolonged shelf life compared to liquid honey.</p></p>
