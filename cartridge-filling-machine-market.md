Semi-automatic machines require some degree of human involvement, such as placing the cartridge in the machine manually, but still offer automated filling processes. Manual machines, on the other hand, rely entirely on human operation, requiring manual cartridge placement and filling. These variations cater to the diverse needs of different industries and businesses involved in the cartridge filling process.</p></p>
