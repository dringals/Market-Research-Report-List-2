<p><p>The Flaring Tool market consists of three main types: the Double Flaring Tool, the Single Flaring Tool, and the Combination Flaring Tool. The Double Flaring Tool is designed for creating double flares on tubing, providing a secure connection. The Single Flaring Tool is used to create a single flare on tubing, ensuring a tight seal. The Combination Flaring Tool combines both the functionality of double and single flaring, offering versatility in various applications. These tools are commonly used in automotive, HVAC, and plumbing industries for tube fabrication and repairs.</p></p>
