<p><p>The golf rangefinders market can be classified into three main types: laser golf rangefinders, GPS golf rangefinders, and others. Laser golf rangefinders utilize a laser beam to measure the distance from the device to a specific target on the golf course. GPS golf rangefinders rely on satellite technology to provide accurate distance readings, along with additional features such as course maps and tracking capabilities. The "others" category encompasses alternative technologies or hybrid options that may combine features from both laser and GPS rangefinders, offering golfers different choices to suit their preferences and needs.</p></p>
