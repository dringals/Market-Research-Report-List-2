<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Welding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inert Protect Type</li><li>Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive welding market includes two main types: Inert Protect Type and Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type. </p><p>Inert Protect Type utilizes gases like helium or argon, which do not react with the welding process, to create a protective environment around the weld pool. This prevents oxidation and ensures high-quality welds. </p><p>On the other hand, Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type uses a mixture of gases, including inert gases like argon and active gases like carbon dioxide. This combination provides better control over the weld pool and helps achieve stronger welds.</p></p>
