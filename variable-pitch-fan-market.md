<p><p>Variable pitch fans are commonly used in industries and commercial settings for their ability to adjust the angle of their blades, thereby controlling the air volume and pressure generated. These fans are available in different market types, namely belt drive, direct drive, and others. Belt drive fans incorporate a system of belts and pulleys to transmit power from the motor to the fan blades, allowing for increased flexibility in fan speed adjustments. Direct drive fans, on the other hand, have a motor directly connected to the fan blades, providing higher energy efficiency and simpler maintenance. Other market types may include specialized fans designed for specific applications such as exhaust fans, ventilation fans, or cooling towers.</p></p>
